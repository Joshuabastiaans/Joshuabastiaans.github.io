WHO I AM

Hi, I'm Joshua. I am a creative game designer with a passion for crafting unique and immersive experiences. Just like other media, games take people outside of their daily lives to escape and connect. My goal is to create games that make you connect and feel what it’s like to be someone else, somewhere else. As a second-year Game Design student at HKU, I'm excited to continue pushing the boundaries of game design and create experiences that resonate deeply with players.

My entire life has been a source of inspiration for my creations. The music I listen to, the movies I watch, and the games I play all significantly influence the games I develop today. When it comes to inspiring my team with a concept, I enjoy creating a simple soundtrack that captures the vibe of the idea. Music conveys emotions and nuances that words often can't describe, and it helps align everyone's perception of the concept, ensuring we're all on the same page.
I also play the drums, and I’ve learned that creating good-sounding drums requires understanding of the entire composition. I believe that the same goes for games. To create effective levels, puzzles or mechanics that suit the game I have to understand the other disciplines that go into the development process of the game. By familiarizing myself with various aspects of the process, I can create more cohesive and engaging experiences.
